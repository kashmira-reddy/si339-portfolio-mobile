Welcome!

Hi! My name is Kashmira Reddy and I'm a junior at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor pursuing a dual degree in Information (BS) and Organizational Studies (BA).

I transferred to the University of Michigan after spending my freshman year at the University of Maryland, College Park. When I moved from my hometown in Maryland to Michigan in 2020, I knew I wanted to study information analysis and I found the School of Information to perfectly fit my goals. I am especially interested in using technology to generate more effective business strategies for reducing bias in the hiring process, innovating desirable products, and maximizing social media outreach in organizations.

After I graduate in August of 2023, I plan to pursue a career in financial technology.
